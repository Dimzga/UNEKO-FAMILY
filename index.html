About

What is AMV?

AMV (Anime Music Video) is a combination of clips from various types of anime that are combined into one that is adapted to music.

What is AMV Team?

AMV Team is a community created to facilitate video makers (editors) to express their creativity in making music videos using a collection of anime pieces.

About Not For Sure Team

This team was created by an AMV editor named Dwii (@elt.dwii) on 25th june 2021, this team uploaded the first MEP on 29 june 2021 which is a collaboration MEP with Kozue Unit, Daddy Style MEP. The name of this team NFS (Not For Sure) is taken from the name of a game, namely Need For Speed.
